# AI Phone Automation Platform

> An AI-powered voice automation platform developed by **Caeloq Studios** to help businesses automate customer calls, appointment scheduling, lead qualification, and customer support through conversational AI.

---

## Overview

The AI Phone Automation Platform is an intelligent virtual receptionist designed to reduce repetitive administrative work while providing businesses with a professional and natural customer experience.

Originally developed for the automotive industry, the platform has evolved into a flexible AI solution capable of serving businesses across multiple industries.

This project combines conversational AI, workflow automation, and modern large language models (LLMs) to create voice agents that can answer questions, qualify leads, schedule appointments, and automate business processes.

---

## Why I Built This

After spending years leading business operations, I saw firsthand how much time businesses lose answering repetitive phone calls and scheduling appointments.

I founded **Caeloq Studios** to build practical AI solutions that eliminate repetitive work, allowing business owners and employees to focus on higher-value tasks while improving the customer experience.

This project represents the first major product under the Caeloq Studios brand.
